Enter your statistics from a pool of starting points. The amount of starting points can vary, but it is usually 30. Statistics are between 3-10 inclusive.

Skills

You automatically get 5 in two skills based on your class. You have 15 skill points to start with to distribute among the other skills. No skill can ever exceed 3 to start with, and no skill can ever exceed 12.
